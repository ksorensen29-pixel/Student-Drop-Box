enter your concerns for your teachers here:


By providing your concerns to your teachers they can better adjust their classes to suit your needs and make a more friendly enviroment. If you have any questions go to this website.

Acceptable inputs

  • Concerns regarding teacher or student behavior.
  • Ways the teacher can make the class more engaging.
  • Thoughts on workload, tests, homework, classwork, etc.
  • Overall thoughts or reviews of the class.
  • Matieral requests, pencils, notebooks, paper, etc.

unacceptable inputs

  • Swear words or slurs.
  • Disrespectful tone or language.
  • Threats or personal situations.
  • Meaningless concerns, asking to never have homework again, no tests, etc
  • Anything not school appropiate.
