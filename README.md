Student Drop Box
Overview

Student Drop Box is a simple communication tool that allows students to submit questions, problems, or concerns directly to their teachers. It aims to improve classroom efficiency by helping teachers address issues outside of lesson time.

Purpose

The goal of the system is to reduce interruptions during class and give students a structured way to communicate academic difficulties, ultimately improving learning outcomes and time management.

Key Idea
Students submit messages or questions through a digital drop box
Teachers review and respond at their convenience
Helps streamline communication between students and teachers
Future Improvements
Anonymous submissions option
File/image attachments for questions
Notification system for responses
Categorization of submissions (homework, general questions, etc.)
